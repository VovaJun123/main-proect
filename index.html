Our Projects

Your home. your way.

Strong Base Carpentry & Joinery is the leading company for luxury custom carpentry and joinery services for the industrial and heritage sectors in Greater London and the Home Counties. Using cutting edge CNC technology, we offer a comprehensive service from conception to completion ensuring a seamless process for our customer. Our highly experienced team of specialised craftsmen have more than 15 years of experience in creating elegant, bespoke doors, windows, staircases and furniture. We take great pride in the reputation we have for exquisite workmanship and attention to detail.

How We Can Help You

We have a workshop dedicated to the creation of our unique products, so everything is fabricated in house. Our use of the latest 3 and 5 CNC axis technologies to improve upon traditional carpentry techniques, allows us to provide precise shapes, high quality finishes and faster execution times at competitive prices, without losing the beauty of the wood itself. ​ We work directly with home owners, as well as in collaboration with architects, interior designers and building companies. Our projects range from small - designing that statement piece for a lounge, to large scale - complete custom design windows and doors for a whole new home extension.
