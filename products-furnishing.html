PRODUCTS & FURNISHING

Dors

Make a statement with your front door, travel from room to room through quality internal doors, or take pleasure in stepping through beautiful French doors out to your patio or garden. If you can imagine it, we can make it. To see some of our favourite creations take a look in our gallery.

Windows

We design and fit high end windows of any style for any room. We also work in collaboration with architects and builders to provide custom made windows for a new home or home extension, whether you are building up or out. See some of our previous projects in our gallery.

Staircases

A staircase is not simply a way to move from level to another. You can create a centrepiece for your home with a curved and sweeping staircase, or combine form and function with a sleek and linear staircase. Why not peruse our gallery for inspiration.

Furniture

Our team can assist you in designing your dream kitchen, the heart of the home. They can offer ideas on how to combine style and storage with custom design media wall units, bedroom wardrobes and bathroom vanities. For something special, ask us about in home bars and our soon to be released bespoke furniture range. Many examples of our work can be found in the gallery.
